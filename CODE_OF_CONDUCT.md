# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

This repository should be a respectful, professional environment for research, engineering, and language-preservation work.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and constructively
- assume good intent while addressing problems directly
- protect contributors, reviewers, and community members from harassment or abuse
- respect Maasai language, culture, and source communities
- avoid posting private, unsafe, or unlicensed material

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory conduct
- abusive language or personal attacks
- publishing private information or credentials
- knowingly submitting ungoverned or unauthorized data
- intentionally bypassing repository security, review, or workflow safeguards

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior threatens the safety, legality, or operational health of the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to repository maintainers through GitHub or another approved NorthernTribe-Research private channel. If the report includes security-sensitive information, follow [SECURITY.md](/home/ntr/Documents/dev/maasai-lang/SECURITY.md) instead of posting publicly.
