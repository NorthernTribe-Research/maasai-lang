## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how LinguaMaster ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at linguamaster.ai and linguamaster.ntr-kenya.com (the "Platform"). This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

### Types of Cookies

**Session Cookies**: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.

**Persistent Cookies**: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.

**First-Party Cookies**: Cookies set by the website you're visiting.

**Third-Party Cookies**: Cookies set by a domain other than the one you're visiting.

## Your Cookie Choices

### Managing Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:

1. **Cookie Banner**: When you first visit the Platform, you'll see a cookie banner where you can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences.

2. **Cookie Settings**: Click the "Cookie Settings" button in the footer to change your preferences at any time.

3. **Browser Settings**: You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent.

### Browser-Specific Instructions

**Google Chrome**:
1. Click the three dots menu → Settings
2. Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
3. Choose your preferred setting

**Mozilla Firefox**:
1. Click the menu button → Settings
2. Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
3. Choose your preferred setting

**Safari**:
1. Safari menu → Preferences
2. Privacy tab
3. Choose your cookie preferences

**Microsoft Edge**:
1. Click the three dots menu → Settings
2. Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
3. Choose your preferred setting

### Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies:

- **Essential Cookies**: The Platform will not function properly. You will not be able to log in or use core features.
- **Functional Cookies**: You will lose personalization features. Your preferences will not be saved.
- **Analytics Cookies**: We will not be able to improve the Platform based on usage data.
- **Marketing Cookies**: You will still see advertisements, but they will not be personalized.
