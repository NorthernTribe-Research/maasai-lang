## How We Store Your Data

### Data Storage

**Location**: Your data is stored on secure servers in the United States (Google Cloud Platform, us-central1 region).

**Security Measures**:
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Secure database access controls
- Regular security audits
- Automated backups
- Access logging and monitoring

**Data Retention Periods**:
- **Active accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive accounts**: Deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Session logs**: Deleted after 90 days
- **Error logs**: Deleted after 1 year
- **Voice recordings**: Deleted after 30 days (unless saved by user)
- **Deleted accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days

### Data Backup

We maintain automated backups of your data for disaster recovery purposes:
- Daily full backups (retained for 30 days)
- Point-in-time recovery (7 days)
- Backups stored in geographically separate location
- Backups encrypted at rest

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Portability
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Download your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
- Request a copy of your data

**How to Exercise**: Go to Settings > Privacy > Download My Data

### Correction and Update
You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Update your profile information
- Modify your preferences

**How to Exercise**: Go to Settings > Profile or Settings > Preferences

### Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
You have the right to:
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Request deletion of specific data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

**How to Exercise**: Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account

**Note**: Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records for 7 years)

### Objection and Restriction
You have the right to:
- Object to certain data processing
- Restrict processing of your data
- Opt out of marketing communications

**How to Exercise**: Go to Settings > Privacy > Data Processing Preferences

### Cookie Management
You have the right to:
- Accept or reject non-essential cookies
- Change cookie preferences at any time
- Use the platform with essential cookies only

**How to Exercise**: Cookie banner on first visit, or Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences

### Data Portability
You have the right to:
- Receive your data in structured, machine-readable format
- Transfer your data to another service

**How to Exercise**: Go to Settings > Privacy > Download My Data

## Children's Privacy

LinguaMaster is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@linguamaster.ai, and we will delete such information.

For users aged 13-18, we recommend parental supervision and consent before using our platform.
